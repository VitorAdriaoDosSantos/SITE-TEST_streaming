Demon Slayer


Set in Japan during the Taishō Period (1912-1926), the story revolves around Tanjirō Kamado, a kind and intelligent boy who lives with his mother and brothers, earning money selling coal, just like his late father. One day, while returning home after going to a town to sell coal, Tanjiro discovers that his entire family has been attacked by oni, and one of his sisters, Nezuko, is the only one who survived the attack. Nezuko then happens to be an oni, but she surprisingly still shows signs of human emotions and thoughts. Tanjirō then decides to become an oni hunter, and with Nezuko's help, goes on journeys across Japan in order to prevent the same tragedy that affected his family from happening to other people, while he searches for a way to make Nezuko human again.
